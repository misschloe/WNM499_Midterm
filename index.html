# CONCEPT

1. (Noun) A playing card with one large figure in its center that can be the card with either the highest or lowest value

2. (Noun) A person who is very skilled at something

3. (Adjective) Very good.

ACE is a typeface inspired Parallel Lines by Kristiina Lahde. The font was developed for visual display purpose to be used for headings and simple typography to assist playing card design. It’s an eye-catching design that looks fantastic used for headlines, posters, playing card and much more.

For all your bold, geometric typeface needs, it's perfectly suited to those times when you need to add impact to your designs.
